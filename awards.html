Awards and accreditations

In order to make sure we're providing the finest service possible, Sterling Staffing periodically evaluates and reviews its operations. Because of our stringent approach, we have won recognition and accreditation in a variety of business sectors, enabling us to fulfil our core goal of leading the field in the delivery of healthcare that is really humane.

ISO:9001: 2012

The continuing provision of excellent service to both staff and clients is everything to us. A quality management system that we use internally was initially certified to ISO 9000 in 1998. Our processes and practises are evolving together with us as we grow, reflecting our commitment to providing top-notch customer service.

The following is a list of the components of the nursing and care staff quality management system:

  • Quality guidelines and policies are available online on a dedicated website.
  • complaint handling procedure
  • Clearly stated hiring procedures and policies
  • Processes for managing employee performance (at all levels)
  • internal review procedure
  • monthly analysis of quality control
  • Identifying staff training and development
  • Review and evaluation of client and patient comments

    The term "continuing professional development," or CPD, is used to refer to the learning activities that professionals take part in to advance and improve their skills.

    Employers across sectors benefit from an organised, pragmatic, and rigorous approach to learning because it helps them keep important employees and build the knowledge and skills necessary for their businesses to maintain a competitive edge.

    Participating in CPD keeps academic and practical qualifications current, enabling people to continuously "up skill" or "re-skill" themselves regardless of their occupation, age, or educational background.
