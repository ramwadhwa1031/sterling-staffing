GDPR COMPLIANCE POLICIES

Sterling Staffing Ltd. is committed to adhering to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Sterling Staffing LTD maintains product features, corporate protocols, and legal documents to help our users and customers comply. The following are some of the frequently asked questions to help our customers, employees, and candidates. The detailed policy can be accessed on the Website.

What data do we collect?

The data Sterling Staffing LTD collects, which is being gathered through the service, consists of any personal, employment, payroll, and location information provided consciously and voluntarily by a customer (employer), an end user, the customerâ€™s administrator, or an employee's use of the Sterling Staffing LTD platform. In addition, the employer might seek location information for timekeeping purposes. Through our hiring platform, employees may also collect information from job-seeking candidates.

What is Sterling Staffing LTD's privacy policy?

Our privacy policy can be accessed here and covers all aspects of GDPR adherence in great detail.

Who is responsible for employee data?

Any Sterling Staffing LTD customer that uses our services to store their employee or candidate information owns the data of all employees. The responsibility of updating and deleting all employee data, therefore, lies with the customer. Sterling Staffing LTD provides our customers with the necessary support (customer support or product features) to carry out any such requests however and whenever the customer wants to.

For how long is the data stored

It depends on the contract with our customer. By default, we store data until it's explicitly removed. But we provide provisions to set up a periodic data removal process for our clients on a contract-to-contract basis. However, we always support data deletion requests sent to support@sterlingstaffing.com for all of our customers. We delete data at the time specified or requested by our customers, with an additional grace period.

Who has access to the data?

Customer representatives who have access to Sterling Staffing LTD services to maintain employee data.

Employees to their data.

Sterling Staffing LTD's internal team only requests data access when a support request is raised by the customer and it is necessary to support such a request.

Who can delete the employee information?

Data retention is governed by the agreement between an employer (customer) and the employee. The customer is thus responsible for any deletion of employee information.

Can the deleted data be reinstated?

An exit action on an employee continues to retain all the employee's information in the system. The actual deletion action is permanent and cannot be reinstated.

Can I delete, edit, view, or access my personal information?

Sterling Staffing LTD is a service provider, and your data provided by the customer (your employer) is owned by the customer, who manages your access. Please contact your employer (the customer) directly to request the deletion or updating of your data during or after your employment.
