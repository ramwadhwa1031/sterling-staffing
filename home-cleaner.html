Care Home Cleaner

Cleanliness and hygiene are extremely important in a nursing home environment. A care home is a safe place for residents to feel at home, and therefore a clean environment is essential for many reasons. In addition to reducing the spread of infection, a clean nursing home can say a thousand words to staff, residents and visitors. Now more than ever, ensuring the hygiene of the premises is an absolute necessity to protect residents, staff and visitors from infectious diseases such as COVID-19.

Investing in cleaning and disinfection can be overwhelming, although the care of elderly residents already requires constant care. This is where Sterling Staffing steps in. If you care about your residents and run your facility smoothly, we can lend a hand with commercial cleaning services for your care and nursing homes. We help keep the premises clean by ensuring they are cleaned to the highest standards and are hygienic and safe for everyone.

Providing a Helping Hand with our Care Home Cleaning Services

Sterling Staffing's effective nursing home disinfection and cleaning services greatly control the spread of infection, leaving the right impression on staff, visitors and residents. Our reliable and dedicated local teams ensure your premises are cleaned to the highest standards so you can focus on providing the best care for your residents. Whether it's upholstery cleaning, deodorizing or full deep cleaning, our professional cleaning teams know how to complete these tasks to the highest standard.

We will work out with you a cleaning schedule suitable for the care home and its needs, minimize disruption and agree on the areas you want us to clean. Sterling Staffing is well equipped to take care of all areas of a nursing home, from common areas, toilets, kitchens, rooms, floors and ceilings.

The Sterling Staffing team offers highly qualified healthcare personnel on demand to clients like care facilities, clinics, and supported living services.
